Pillers of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the practical implementation of ideas that result in the introduction of new goods or services or improvement in offering goods or services.

Sustainability

Sustainability means meeting our own needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.

Education

The process of receiving or giving systematic instruction, especially at a school or university.

Empowerment

The process of becoming stronger and more confident, especially in controlling one's life and claiming one's rights.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Safety of environment needs a new life-style. That life-style would not be imposed, it will be a choice.Young people love that life style as a choice. Each young person will grow up as a three zero person -zero net carbon emissions, zero wealth concentration,through building social businesses only, and zero unemployment by turning themselves into entrepreneurs. Each person will grow up as a three zero person, and remain a three zero person all his/her life. That will create the new civilisation."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
